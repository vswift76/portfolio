About Me

Wife, Mother, Sagittarian, Dragon, I am all of that and more. By night, I work for a local Amazon FC. Inbound is my home department, but I'm crosstrained all over the building so I never know where I'll end up throughout the night. During the day, when I'm not catching up on my beauty sleep, taking care of my three kids (my husband totally counts, he said so himself, so that makes 3) and the two cats, I'm learning how to be a front end web developer. I also admin a FB fan page for the Swedish metal band Avatar. I am quite well known as dressing up as AVATAR's vocalist Johannes Eckerström for shows and have given myself the title of Lady Gin Swift, Bard of the Freakshow. I also stream on Twitch from time to time, you can watch me yell at games or when my husband and I get with our group of friends and stream during what we call the Metal Meal Meltdown, a weeklong stream marathon raising money for St. Jude Children's Research Hospital (more info can be found on my Raven Wine project) I am wrapping up a Javascript class through Code Louisville right now, this is my second Code Louisville course (the first being front end web development) I am currently trying to decide my next Code Louisville course, leaning toward maybe taking a full stack Javascript course.
